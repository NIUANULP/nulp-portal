Instructions
Guidelines before uploading any template:

1. The dimension of the template should be 3300x2246 pixels.
2. It is suggested to assign a unique name for the uploaded certificate template.
3. The template should be in jpg or jpeg format.
4. The certificate that will be issued to a user will have 3 placeholders/variables as shown below:



5. The location of <Name of the User>, <Course Name> and <Completion Date> will be fixed on the final certificate, only those will be replaced with actual values.
6. You have the flexibility to change the background colour, logo and text keeping in mind that the placeholder's(<Name of the User>, <Course Name> and <Completion Date>) location, which is fixed.
7. Considering the top left corner as (0,0) coordinate and bottom right corner as (3300, 2246), the <Name of the User> will be centered on coordinate (1650, 1220), <Course Name> on (1650, 1630) and <Completion Date> on (1650, 1990) approximately.
