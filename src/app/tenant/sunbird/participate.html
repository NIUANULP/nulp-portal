Participate in the Learnathon?

Learnathon invites the participation under the following two categories, Group Participation, and Institutional Participation.
CATEGORY 1

Group

Under the group category, you can register as the following
  • City Body: ULBs, SPVs, Parastatal bodies or any other administrative body.
  • Academia & CSOs: Universities, Colleges, Research Orgs., Think Tanks, Training Entities)
  • Industries: PMCs, PMUs, Start-Ups or any other private vendor working with the ULBs.
QUESTION 1
What is a solution implemented by your city that has benefited the department / citizens?
QUESTION2
How do you solve any on-ground challenge (any of the 10 themes) that can be adopted by an ULB and enhance their skills?

CATEGORY 1

Individual

Under the individual category, you can participate in the learnathon by registering yourself as either an Urban Scholar, or a Government Official.
