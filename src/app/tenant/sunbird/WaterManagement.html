Water Supply and Management

Water Supply management has become a challenging task owing to the sudden rise in urban population. By following national reforms & policies, ULBs are trying to improve service coverage and water quality. However, due to inadequate capacities urban practitioners are grappling with the bridging of demand-supply gap. Under the following sub-categories, submit solutions that can be easily replicated
  • 24 X 7 Water Supply
  • Smart solutions under Water Management
  • Augmentation / Refurbishment of Water Pipelines
  • Rainwater Harvesting
