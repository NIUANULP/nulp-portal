What is Learnathon?

The biggest urban learning challenge is launched to announce to the urban ecosystem the valid intent of the National Urban Learning Platform (NULP). To build India’s largest urban learning community by curating and collating the best content on the platform, the learnathon will recognize champions in the urban ecosystem across ten diverse themes . By participating in any of the categories, innovative solutions will be shared on NULP as a response to solve the ever-evolving urban challenges in India. Read more to participate in “TARL 2023” and become an integral part of India’s largest urban learning community
